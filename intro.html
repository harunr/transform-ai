The first edition of Transform.AI took place June 14th and 15th 2017 in Paris. 130 participants from Mexico to New Zealand gathered together over two days to explore the business implications of Artificial Intelligence. The aim of the event was to provide a platform for senior executives to gain an in depth understanding of the real impact of this fast growing, transformational technology and how to apply it to business.
